Chocolate Cake

Description

My mum's chocolate cake recipe

Ingredients

  • 8oz butter
  • 8oz sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 8oz flour
  • Baking powder
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat fan oven to 180C
  2. Cream butter and sugar
  3. Mix in eggs
  4. Sift and mix in flour, baking powder and cocoa powder
  5. Add vanilla extract
  6. Bake for 20 minutes
  7. Leave to cool
